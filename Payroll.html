Payroll Services

At ARSI Consulting LLP, we offer comprehensive payroll services designed to ensure accurate, timely, and compliant payroll processing.
Our goal is to simplify the complexities of payroll management, allowing you to focus on your core business activities. Our payroll services include:

Salary and Wage Processing

01

Accurate Calculations:

We calculate employee salaries, wages, bonuses, commissions, and overtime pay accurately, ensuring compliance with employment agreements and labor laws.

02

Timely Disbursements:

We ensure that employees are paid on time, using the most convenient and secure payment methods, such as direct deposits, checks, or payroll cards.

Tax Compliance

Withholding and Reporting

We handle all aspects of payroll tax withholding, including federal, state, and local taxes. Our team ensures that withholdings are accurate and reported correctly.

Tax Filings

We prepare and file all necessary payroll tax returns and reports, including Form 941, Form 940, state unemployment tax returns, and W-2s. Our proactive approach helps prevent penalties and ensures compliance with tax regulations.

End-of-Year Reporting

We manage the preparation and distribution of W-2 and 1099 forms to employees and contractors, ensuring accurate reporting of annual earnings.

Benefits Administration

  • Employee Benefits Management: We manage employee benefits programs, including health insurance, retirement plans, and other perks. This includes enrolling employees, managing contributions, and handling benefit-related inquiries.
  • Deductions Management: We accurately calculate and deduct employee contributions for benefits, such as health insurance premiums and retirement plan contributions, ensuring compliance with plan rules and regulations.

Time and Attendance Management:

Time Tracking:

We implement and manage time tracking systems to accurately record employee hours, including regular time, overtime, and paid time off (PTO).

Attendance Records:

We maintain detailed attendance records, ensuring that employees' work hours are accurately captured and reflected in payroll calculations.

Leave Management:

We manage various types of employee leave, such as vacation, sick leave, and family leave, ensuring compliance with company policies and legal requirements.

Compliance and Reporting

  • Regulatory Compliance: We manage employee benefits programs, including health insurance, retirement plans, and other perks. This includes enrolling employees, managing contributions, and handling benefit-related inquiries.
  • Audit Support: We provide support during payroll audits, supplying necessary documentation and explanations to auditors.
  • Custom Reports: We generate detailed payroll reports tailored to your specific needs, providing insights into payroll expenses, tax liabilities, and employee compensation.

Employee Self-Service

  • Online Portal: We offer an employee self-service portal where employees can access their pay stubs, tax forms, and personal information. This portal enhances transparency and allows employees to manage their own information.
  • Mobile Access: Employees can access their payroll information from anywhere using mobile devices, improving convenience and accessibility.
