Transaction Advisory Services

At ARSI Consulting LLP, we provide comprehensive transaction advisory services to assist businesses in navigating complex transactions and achieving their strategic objectives. Our team of experienced professionals offers expert guidance and support throughout all stages of the transaction lifecycle. Our transaction advisory services include:

Mergers and Acquisitions (M&A)

  • Buy-Side Advisory: We assist buyers in identifying potential acquisition targets, conducting due diligence, and structuring and negotiating transactions to maximize value and minimize risks.
  • Sell-Side Advisory: We help sellers prepare for and execute the sale of their business or assets, conducting valuation assessments, preparing marketing materials, and negotiating favorable terms.
  • Transaction Structuring: We advise on the optimal deal structure to achieve strategic objectives, minimize tax liabilities, and mitigate risks for all parties involved.
  • Integration Planning: We support post-merger integration efforts, ensuring a smooth transition and maximizing synergies between the merging entities.

Capital Raising and Financing

01

Debt Financing:

We assist businesses in securing debt financing from banks, financial institutions, or private lenders, helping to structure loan agreements and negotiate favorable terms.

02

Equity Financing:

We advise on equity financing options, including private placements, venture capital, and private equity investments, assisting with investor presentations, due diligence, and negotiations.

03

Alternative Financing:

We explore alternative financing options, such as mezzanine financing, convertible debt, and crowdfunding, to meet the unique needs of businesses seeking capital.

Restructuring and Turnaround

  • Financial Restructuring: We help distressed companies restructure their finances, debt, and operations to improve liquidity, reduce costs, and restore profitability.
  • Operational Restructuring: We assess underperforming business units and operations, identifying opportunities to streamline processes, optimize resources, and enhance efficiency.
  • Turnaround Strategy: We develop and implement turnaround strategies to stabilize distressed businesses, improve cash flow, and position them for long-term success.

Due Diligence

Financial Due Diligence:

We conduct comprehensive financial due diligence to assess the financial health and performance of target companies, identifying risks, opportunities, and potential deal-breakers.

Operational Due Diligence:

We evaluate operational aspects of target companies, including systems, processes, and personnel, to identify areas for improvement and integration challenges.

Legal and Regulatory Due Diligence:

We assess legal and regulatory risks associated with transactions, ensuring compliance with laws, regulations, and contractual obligations.

Valuation Services

  • Financial Modeling: We provide independent valuation assessments for businesses, assets, and intellectual property, using a variety of valuation methodologies and approaches to determine fair market value.
  • Fairness Opinions: We develop detailed financial models to analyze the financial impact of transactions, including revenue projections, cost synergies, and investment returns.
  • Business Valuation: We offer fairness opinions to provide an impartial assessment of the fairness of proposed transactions to shareholders and other stakeholders.
