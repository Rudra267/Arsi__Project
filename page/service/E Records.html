E-Records Management Services

In today's digital age, managing electronic records efficiently and securely is crucial for businesses to ensure compliance, streamline operations, and protect sensitive information. At ARSI Consulting LLP, we offer comprehensive e-records management services to help organizations effectively manage their electronic records throughout their lifecycle. Our e-records management services include:

Records Inventory and Assessment

  • Inventory Analysis: We conduct a comprehensive inventory of your electronic records, identifying all types of electronic documents, files, and data sources across your organization.
  • Records Classification: We classify electronic records based on their content, importance, and retention requirements, ensuring that each record is appropriately categorized for management and retention purposes.

Records Retention and Disposition

01

Retention Schedule Development:

We develop customized records retention schedules tailored to your organization's specific legal, regulatory, and operational requirements.

02

Disposition Management:

We establish processes and protocols for the secure and compliant disposal of electronic records at the end of their retention period, ensuring data privacy and confidentiality..

Electronic Document Management Systems (EDMS)

  • EDMS Implementation: We assist in selecting, configuring, and implementing electronic document management systems (EDMS) to centralize the storage, organization, and retrieval of electronic records.
  • Metadata Management: We establish metadata standards and protocols to ensure consistent and accurate indexing and retrieval of electronic records within the EDMS.

Information Governance

Policy Development:

We develop comprehensive information governance policies and procedures to govern the creation, access, use, and disposition of electronic records throughout their lifecycle..

Compliance Monitoring:

We establish mechanisms for monitoring compliance with information governance policies and procedures, conducting regular audits and reviews to ensure adherence to established standards.

Security and Access Controls

  • Access Control Policies: We establish access control policies and procedures to govern who can access, modify, and delete electronic records, ensuring data security and confidentiality.
  • Encryption and Data Protection: We implement encryption and other data protection measures to safeguard electronic records from unauthorized access, breaches, and cyber threats.

Records Preservation and Archiving

  • Digital Preservation Strategies: We develop strategies for the long-term preservation and archiving of electronic records, ensuring their integrity, authenticity, and accessibility over time.
  • Archival Systems: We implement archival systems and repositories to store and manage electronic records that have long-term or permanent retention requirements, such as historical records and institutional memory.

Compliance and Legal Support

  • Regulatory Compliance: We ensure compliance with relevant laws, regulations, and industry standards governing the management of electronic records, including data privacy laws, e-discovery requirements, and industry-specific regulations.
  • Litigation Support: We provide litigation support services, including electronic discovery (e-discovery) and records retrieval, to assist organizations in responding to legal demands and regulatory inquiries.
